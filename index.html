Supplementary figures for "Noisy networks are graphs, too", 2020



Real data, such as from the structural brain network, is often assumed to consist of strong, likely real, edges and weak edges that could possibly be noise. In our project, we will add noise to a model network (navy) that has been thresholded to a specified edge density œÅT (rose). Finally, we will measure the structure of the noisy edges (gold).
